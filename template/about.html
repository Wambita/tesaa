About Tesaa

Bridging the gap between small businesses and microfinance institutions

Our Mission

At Tesaa, we believe in the power of microfinance to transform lives and communities. Our mission is to create a seamless connection between small businesses seeking financial support and microfinance institutions ready to invest in their growth.

Empowering Entrepreneurs

We provide small business owners with access to the financial resources they need to turn their dreams into reality.

Facilitating Growth

By connecting businesses with microfinance institutions, we foster economic growth and job creation in communities around the world.

Promoting Financial Inclusion

Our platform helps bridge the gap in financial services, making them accessible to those who need them most.

How It Works

  1. Small businesses create profiles and submit loan applications
  2. Microfinance institutions review applications and offer tailored loans
  3. Businesses compare offers and choose the best fit
  4. Funds are disbursed, and repayments are managed through our platform
  5. Both parties benefit from a transparent, efficient process
